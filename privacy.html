Privacy Policy for Puntje

Last updated: February 1, 2024

This privacy policy outlines how AAJ Consultancy (the creators of Puntje) collects, uses, and protects your data. By using the Puntje app, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Information Collection and Use

We collect personal information such as your email address and first name. This information is gathered through account registration and app usage. We use this information to personalize the application, making it useful by creating your profile and assigning points to it.

Data Sharing and Analytics

Your data may be shared for analytics purposes to understand app usage and improve the application.

Data Retention

Your data is retained until you decide to delete your account or until the group associated with your account is removed.

Your Rights

You have the right to delete your account and, consequently, your profile information. A feature will soon be available in the profile page allowing you to delete your account and data.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for data storage and notifications. We recommend you review our privacy policy to understand how your data is handled.

Children's Privacy

Our application is designed for a general audience and does not specifically target children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Users will be informed of any changes to this privacy policy. We reserve the right to update our privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will post a notification on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contact@puntje.app.
